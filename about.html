About Us

We are a dedicated team of neuroscientists, software engineers, and data visualization experts committed to advancing cognitive neuroscience research through innovative tools and technologies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to advanced neuroscience analysis tools by creating intuitive, powerful software that helps researchers unlock new insights into brain function and cognition. We believe that cutting-edge research tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their programming background.

Core Values

  • Open Science: We believe in transparency and collaboration in scientific research
  • User-Centered Design: Our tools are built with researchers' needs at the forefront
  • Innovation: We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in neuroscience visualization
  • Community: We foster a collaborative environment where researchers can share and learn
